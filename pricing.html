Do you have any other questions?

1.What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day refund policy for all subscriptions. If you are not satisfied with Clickrank.ai within the first 7 days of purchase, you can request a full refund, no questions asked.

2. What happens to my optimizations if I cancel my subscription?

If your subscription is terminated, all saved optimizations will be lost due to the disconnection of the JavaScript connection. We recommend exporting your data or applying changes permanently before ending your subscription.

3. Can I register the same website on multiple accounts?

No, each website can only be registered once and cannot be accessed from two different Clickrank.ai accounts. This policy ensures the integrity and security of your website’s SEO data and settings.

4. Is my Google Search Console data safe with Clickrank.ai?

Yes, your Google Search Console data is completely safe. We do not store any of your data on our servers. All data is fetched directly into your user session from Google's servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
